Committing to provide decent social housing for our people.

We specialize in social housing-based rental accommodation for the low to the middle-income category by working with the government and other stakeholders. The aim is to eliminate the issue of the severe housing shortage.

Who we are

Buhlebezwe Property NPC is a ‘Not for profit’ Company which was set up in 2015 by Mr Madoda Blose. It was started to undertake the shortage of social housing stock in South Africa. Buhlebezwe is currently accredited by Social Housing Regulatory Authority (SHRA).

Developing

We acts as a developer by appointing contractors and professionals to build (in some cases refurbish) suitable housing.

Management

We manage our own rental housing stock. We will manage own rental housing stock. The rental housing portfolio will include newly built stock and existing public rental stock that will be transferred from the Municipalities.

Our goal

BUHLEBEZWE PROPERTY NPC targets to create 5000 rental units within the next 5 years all predominantly within the Kwa-Zulu Natal region as well as Gauteng, and provide a quality customer service to the tenants taking residency in those unit.

Our Strategic Framework

What our initiate support, how it will support the overall projects as well as the key objectives of stakeholders.

Our Vision

To be among the leading broad-based black controlled entities providing innovative social housing solutions in South Africa.

Our Mission

To be a leading provider of social housing in SA. To provide a professional and efficient service, managing properties to achieve optimal rental income whilst creatively serving the interests of tenants and stakeholders.

Our Values

Our actions aim to service society, care for communities and the environment in which we operate in, and providing dignified and affordable social housing.

Collaboration

No-one here is on their own. We’re one company with a joint ambition – all working together to make decisions and get things done more effectively.

Accountability

We take ownership of our decisions, our actions and our results. We will deliver on our promises and acknowledge our mistakes. Above all else, we will never pass on the blame.

Innovation

Challenging the way things have always been done is a key priority for us. By actively developing new solutions, encouraging new ways of thinking and finding new ways of working, we’re dedicated on improving business, while creating more sustainable homes for a South Africans to enjoy.
